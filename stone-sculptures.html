stone sculptures

I have begun sculpting stone. I currently have a few pieces that I've completed and some in progress.

001 - abstract marble

this was my first piece I made. I learned a lot about working with smaller pieces, filing stone down, and sanding. it now resides somewhere in my parent's house

002 - alabaster love

my second piece was made out of alabaster. alabaster is really soft and I learned a lot about how easy it is to bruise stone with chisels. this one is in my flat

003 - relaxed nude

the third piece I made is a larger marble sculpture. I was still learning about bruising stone with chisels and this one has very clear signs in the tight areas. this one lives in my flat

004 - alabaster love 2

this one is uh... probably my best work but also the one I don't like to show people? it's made from the same alabaster as the second piece and they kinda fit together. it's hidden in my flat

005 - migraine

this is my biggest piece yet. I started this piece without knowing where I was going. it's still a work in progress but it's almost complete (as of june 2025). I BELIEVE this is limestone, but it might be alabaster.
