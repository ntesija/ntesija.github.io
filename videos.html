videos

I often walk around with my hi8 video camera and film random things. I try to capture the essence of old family videos.

Sonder

I made a movie called Sonder. It follows 8 people through San Francisco as they film parts of their day. It arose from me wanting to see what other people were doing with their days as I was feeling like I was not doing enough. It was really uplifting to see that everyone else was doing just about the same things I was doing too.

A trip to Bristol

This is a video of a trip I took to Bristol. I have moved to London and am enjoying exploring the surrounding countries. I added some very simple music over top of the video because it felt like it needed some ambient music.
